It's not magic — It's QuickCook

Hello, We're QuickFood, a website that turns your chosen ingredients into delicious recipes. You don't have to be a chef, just follow the steps below and enjoy your meal!

Step 1

Simply choose the ingredient(s) you wish to make use of. These could be anything in your kitchen (yes, including spices).

Step 2

Now enter those ingredient(s) into the search bar one after the other, seperated by a coma for some magic.

Step 3

Simply choose the ingredient(s) you wish to make use of and enter them into the search bar one after the other, with a coma seperating each ingredient.

Enjoy !
